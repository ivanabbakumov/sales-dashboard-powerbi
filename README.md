# 📊 Sales Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Overview

This project is a Power BI dashboard designed to analyze sales performance across different regions, categories, and time periods.

The dashboard provides insights into revenue, order volume, profitability, and product performance.

---

[sales_dashboard.pdf](https://github.com/user-attachments/files/26243848/sales_dashboard.pdf)

---

## 🎯 Business Goals

* Analyze overall sales performance
* Identify top-performing products
* Compare regions and categories
* Evaluate profitability (Profit & Margin)
* Track sales trends over time

---

## 📊 Key Metrics

* **Total Sales**
* **Total Quantity**
* **Number of Orders**
* **Total Profit**
* **Profit Margin (%)**

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

* Sales trend over time (monthly analysis)
* Top 10 products by revenue
* Sales breakdown by region
* Sales breakdown by category
* Interactive filtering by region

---

## 🛠 Tools Used

* Power BI
* DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
* Data Cleaning (Power Query)

---

## 💡 Key Insights

* Sales show a consistent upward trend over time
* Certain regions significantly outperform others
* Top products contribute a large share of revenue
* Profit margin remains stable around ~18%
